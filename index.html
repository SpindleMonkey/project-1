Red Rover!

Remember that game from back in the day? You and your friends split into two teams and tried to break through the other team's line? Now you can play from the comfort of your very own recliner!

How to play

  • When your team is up, you'll be asked to select a player from a dropdown (the player will be from the other team); this is the player you'll ask for when you call 'Red Rover, Red Rover, send so-and-so on over!'
  • The other team will then be prompted to send their player over
  • The other team's player will then scramble across the field towards your team and try to break through the linked arms of 2 of your players
  • If your team prevents the other team's player from breaking through, you get to add that player to your team!
  • However, if the other team's player breaks through, the other team keeps their player and picks one of your players to add to their team :(
  • Repeat the above steps until either one team has all the players, or there's only 1 player left on the team that's up (1 player, unfortunately, cannot keep the other team from breaking through because there's nobody for that lone player to link arms with!)
